Few Words About Us

I.T.I.C (Informative Teachings about the Internet and Computers) is an informative group that helps people when it comes to the use of internet, computers, gadgets etc. In this time where most of the people are already diving in through the surface of the internet, there's a lot of misfortune that people can encounter problems like viruses, hackers etc. Therefore, that is where we come in to help and guide you to prevent those unlikely things to happen and to ensure your safety and privacy while enjoying the fascinating world of internet.

Our Mission

Mission: To help and guide the people who are using the internet to be aware of the dangers and malicious content around the said technology.

Our Vision

To lessen and prevent bad things happen and to make our web world more friendly around the internet.

What Do We Offer

We give information on how other people can harm and misinform you around the internet. We can also show how to avoid some of the viruses and risks that they may face while using the said technology so that we can assist them in staying safe while using technology and being aware of the viruses and dangers that they may encounter.
